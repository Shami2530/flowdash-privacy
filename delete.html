🗑️ Account & Data Deletion
Delete Your Account
Last updated: May 2026  ·  In compliance with Google Play Data Safety requirements

× All your expenses, budgets and goals will be deleted

📦
What data gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days:

  • Account profile — name, username, email address
  • All financial data — expenses, budget categories, monthly limits
  • All savings goals and progress history
  • All scheduled events and calendar data
  • Daily streak, achievements, and badges
  • Notification preferences and app settings
  • Profile photo uploaded to our servers

Note: You can export all your data as a CSV file before deleting your account. The export option is available inside the app at Profile → Delete Account → Export data first.

📱
Method 1 — Delete directly in the app
Delete in Flowdash app Recommended · Instant

Open the Flowdash app and follow these steps:

1
Open the Flowdash app and go to the Profile tab (bottom right)
2
Scroll down to Danger Zone section
3
Tap Delete Account
4
Optionally tap Export data first to save a CSV of all your data
5
Check the confirmation box and tap Continue
6
Enter your password to confirm and tap Delete My Account Forever
7
Your account and all data is permanently deleted immediately
