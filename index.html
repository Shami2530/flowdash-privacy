Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026  ·  Effective for all Flowdash users  ·  App version 1.0.0
1
Information We Collect
We collect the following information when you use Flowdash:
  • Account information — your full name, username, and email address when you register
  • Financial data — expenses, budget categories, savings goals, and spending amounts you enter
  • Schedule data — events and reminders you create
  • Profile photo — if you choose to upload one
  • Device information — device identifiers collected by Google AdMob for advertising purposes
We do not collect any information without your knowledge or consent.
2
How We Use Your Information
Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Flowdash experience:
  • To display your financial insights, budget tracking, and goal progress
  • To send you notifications about events, budget alerts, and streaks
  • To authenticate your account securely
  • To serve advertisements to free users through Google AdMob
We never sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.
3
Data Storage & Security
Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with row-level security policies — meaning only you can access your own data. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS encryption. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or modification.
4
Advertising & Google AdMob
Flowdash displays advertisements to free users through Google AdMob. Google AdMob and its partners may collect and process the following data to serve relevant advertisements:
  • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID / GAID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
You can opt out of personalized ads at any time:
Go to Phone Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Google's privacy policy applies to data collected through AdMob: policies.google.com/privacy

Pro subscribers do not see any advertisements.
5
Data Retention & Deletion
You may delete your account and all associated data at any time through Profile → Settings → Delete Account inside the app. Upon deletion:
  • All your personal data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days
  • All financial data, goals, events, and account information is deleted
  • This action cannot be undone
You can also export your data as a CSV file before deleting your account.
6
Third-Party Services
Flowdash uses the following third-party services:
  • Supabase — backend infrastructure, authentication, and database storage
  • Google AdMob — advertising for free users
No advertising networks have access to your financial or personal data beyond what is described in Section 4.
7
Children's Privacy
Flowdash is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at privacy@inncatechnologies.com and we will delete the information promptly.
8
Your Rights
You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
  • Access — view all your data inside the app at any time
  • Correction — update your name, username, email, and profile photo in Profile settings
  • Deletion — permanently delete your account and all data via the app
  • Export — download a CSV of all your data before deletion
  • Opt-out of ads — disable personalized ads through device settings
For any data requests, contact us at privacy@inncatechnologies.com.
9
Data Safety (Google Play)
In compliance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, here is a summary of data we collect and how it is handled:
  • Name, email, username — collected, encrypted in transit, not shared with third parties
  • Financial data (expenses, budgets, goals) — collected for app functionality, encrypted, not shared
  • Device identifiers — collected by Google AdMob for advertising only
  • App interactions — collected by Google AdMob for advertising only
All data is encrypted in transit. Account data can be deleted at any time via the app.
10
Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Flowdash after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
